About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a four-member group consisting of Jennie, Lisa, Jisoo and Rosé. They are the newest girl group from YG Entertainment after seven years following 2NE1. A team of pretty visuals as well as talented and artistic individuals, they were first introduced in 2012 with “Who’s That Girl???” teaser series. With most members spending their childhood in foreign countries, the girls are fluent in Korean, English and Japanese, adding up their international appeal suggesting that they were formed to conquer not just the Korean music industry but the global stage as well!

BLACKPINK debuted on August 8th of 2016 with single album “SQUARE ONE” which includes dual title tracks ‘BOOMBAYAH’ and ‘WHISTLE’. ‘WHISTLE’ is a catchy hip-hop song that presents BLACKPINK’s groovy rap and soulful vocals while ‘BOOMBAYAH’ is EDM (cross-over) combined with electronic house and dance hall, a mega-trend genre that is sweeping American Billboard recently.

On November 1, 2016, BLACKPINK returned with the single album “SQUARE TWO” featuring two title tracks ‘PLAYING WITH FIRE’, a tropical house genre with medium tempo & a strong main synth sound and ‘STAY’, a song that boasts tender harmonica sound and sentimental guitar melody.

As a surprise gift to fans while the group is preparing for “SQUARE THREE’ album, BLACKPINK came back into the KPOP scene dropping the single ‘AS IF IT’S YOUR LAST’ which is like a cool drink that will take away the summer heat. It’s a fun, cute and BLACKPINK’s fastest track with lyrics talking about the excitement of love.
